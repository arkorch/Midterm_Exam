A) Argument in favour of the opinion

Open plan offices cana incorporate a lot more desks than cubicle type planning. There is a grand feeling of a larger room compared to claustrophobic cubicles. Equality is brought about amoing the employees as everyone gets the same desk. This design makes collaborations easier.

B) Argument Against the opinion

With a closed office layout each employee will have his or her individual working area. Since all employees are segregated in different rooms, there are less distractions. Reduced noise levels is another advantage of closed office spaces. Overall, security and employee hierarchy is better maintained in cubicle type offices. These are crutial to many industries.
